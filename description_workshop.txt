[h2]Overview[/h2] [img]https://i.imgur.com/jdunrUl.png[/img]
Remnants is the only mod of its kind - a complete reconstruction of your Terraria world that pushes procedural generation to groundbreaking levels. Journey through a once familiar, now foreign land, rich with new environments to explore and secrets to discover.

[h2]Compatibility[/h2] [img]https://i.imgur.com/jdunrUl.png[/img]
Due to the nature of this mod, compatibility with other mods is not guaranteed. Refer to the comprehensive list below when building your modpack.

Supported mods have been built around to ensure an ideal experience: [list]
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3449156562][b]Calamity Fables[/b][/url]
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3449158983][b]Spirit Reforged[/b][/url]
[/list]

Fully compatible mods should be completely safe to use: [list]
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2909886416][b]Thorium Mod[/b][/url]
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2893332653][b]Mod of Redemption[/b][/url]
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2982372319][b]Spirit Classic[/b][/url]
[/list]

Partially compatible mods will cause some minor problems, but otherwise may still be playable: [list]
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2824688072][b]Calamity Mod[/b][/url] (The Sulphurous Sea has a few visual bugs)
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2815540735][b]Fargo's Souls[/b][/url] (The Cursed Coffin arena will not generate)
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3015412343][b]Infernum Mode[/b][/url] (The Golem rework does not suit the new arena)
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2563862309][b]The Stars Above[/b][/url] (The Neon Veil overlaps other structures)
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2984622072][b]Starlight River[/b][/url] (A few biome and structure conflicts)
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3019925104][b]Lunar Veil[/b][/url] (Numerous biome and structure conflicts)
[/list]

Incompatible mods will result in a guaranteed crash or unplayable world: [list]
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2843112914][b]Secrets of the Shadows[/b][/url]
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2807309918][b]Start With Base[/b][/url]
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2930931197][b]Homeward Journey[/b][/url]
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2833852432][b]Spooky Mod[/b][/url]
[/list]

Note that the compatibility of these mods are evaluated only by their individual changes, and not that of any dependencies they might have.
